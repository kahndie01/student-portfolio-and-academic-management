A little about how I got here

I studied Computer Science at Oduduwa University, graduating in 2021, and since then I've worked as a Personal Assistant, Social Media Strategist, and now Executive Secretary. Alongside that day-to-day work, I'm building two ventures of my own: an animated digital invitation studio for Nigerian celebrations, and freelance work in executive support and AI data annotation.

This site brings both sides together — a portfolio of what I've built, and a planner I actually use to keep my academic and personal projects on track. Have a look around, and feel free to send a message from the contact page.
